Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick, clear answers to common queries about our services, processes, and fees.

HomeFAQ
Income Tax
What documents are needed for ITR filing?
You will need Form 16 (for salaried employees), bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D, 80G), capital gains statements if applicable, Aadhaar, PAN card, and last year's ITR acknowledgement. We provide a detailed checklist post-registration.
Can you handle income tax notices on my behalf?
Yes, we handle all income tax notices including scrutiny assessments (143(3)), CPC intimations (143(1)), demand notices, and penalty orders. We prepare detailed replies and represent clients before Assessing Officers.
Should I choose the New or Old Tax Regime?
The optimal choice depends on your income level and available deductions. For incomes below ₹7 lakh, the new regime is often better. For higher incomes with large deductions (HRA, home loan, 80C), the old regime may save more. We do a personalised analysis for every client.
GST
How long does GST registration take?
GST registration is typically granted within 3–7 working days after submission of complete documents. An ARN (Application Reference Number) is generated immediately upon submission, which can be used for business purposes.
Is GST registration mandatory for my business?
GST registration is mandatory if your aggregate turnover exceeds ₹40 lakh (goods) or ₹20 lakh (services) in a financial year. Businesses involved in inter-state supply, e-commerce, or as notified persons must register regardless of turnover.
Business Registration
What is the best business structure for a startup?
For most startups seeking investment, a Private Limited Company is recommended due to its credibility, ability to issue equity, and access to startup tax benefits (Section 80-IAC). LLPs are better for professional services firms with fewer compliance requirements. We advise based on your specific business plan.
How long does company incorporation take?
With complete documents and name approval, a Private Limited Company can be incorporated within 7–10 working days. We handle the entire process — DIN, DSC, name reservation, MOA/AOA drafting, SPICe+ filing, and PAN/TAN application.
NRI & International
Do you serve NRI clients outside India?
Yes, we serve NRI clients across the USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, and other countries entirely remotely. Services include NRI ITR filing, DTAA benefit claims, TDS certificates, capital gains advisory, and FEMA compliance for property transactions and repatriation.
General
How do I securely share documents with you?
We use a secure encrypted client portal for document exchange. You can also share via encrypted email or WhatsApp. Physical documents can be submitted at our Mumbai or Pune offices. All documents are stored with strict confidentiality.
What are your fees, and are there hidden charges?
All fees are disclosed upfront after an initial free consultation. Individual ITR starts from ₹1,500; business compliance packages from ₹8,000/quarter. There are absolutely no hidden charges. We believe in transparent, value-based pricing.
